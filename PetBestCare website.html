Our Story

Built by Pet Lovers,
For Pet Lovers

PawPal was born in 2020 when our team of vets, designers, and animal-welfare advocates grew frustrated with how fragmented pet care had become. We built one platform that brings adoption, expert vet care, and AI guidance together.

  • ✓ Licensed vet partnerships across 30+ cities worldwide
  • ✓ AI trained on 12 million real pet health consultations
  • ✓ Every rescue pet receives a free first vet visit
  • ✓ Carbon-neutral delivery on all shop orders
